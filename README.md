# Lock In - AI personalized study tracker

**Lock In** is a web-based personalized study tracker designed to help students organize and optimize their learning. This platform adapts study plans based on individual preferences, available time, and progress tracking, making it easier to stay on top of learning goals.  


<b>Problem</b> : Many students struggle with **time management** and **efficient study planning**, especially when juggling multiple subjects, assignments, and exams. Traditional static timetables fail to adapt to a student’s progress, leading to missed deadlines or inefficient study sessions.  

- Dynamic study plan generation tailored to the student’s schedule.  
- Real-time progress tracking for motivation and accountability.  
- Easy-to-use interface for managing tasks and subjects in one place.
